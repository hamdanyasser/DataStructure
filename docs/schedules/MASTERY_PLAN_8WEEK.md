## ðŸŽ¯ Study Philosophy

This plan follows proven learning science principles:

1. **Spaced Repetition:** Review topics at increasing intervals for long-term retention
2. **Incremental Difficulty:** Master basics before advancing
3. **Deep Practice:** Understand WHY, not just HOW
4. **Consistent Daily Study:** Better than weekend cramming
5. **Active Coding:** Build muscle memory through repetition
6. **Self-Testing:** Regular quizzes and mock exams
